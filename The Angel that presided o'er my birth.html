Analysis

The Angel That Presided o'er my birth

The poem presents a celestial guardian instructing a newborn to embrace love without external assistance. This theme of unfettered love echoes the Romantic era's emphasis on individualism and the inherent goodness of humanity.

The Angelthat presided o'er my birth

said, 'Little Creature, form'd of Joy and Mirth,

'Go Love without the help of any thing on earth'
