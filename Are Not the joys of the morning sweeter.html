Are Not the joys of morning sweeter

"Are Not the joys of morning sweeter" is a poem by William Blake that captures the contrast between the fresh vitality of morning and the weariness of evening. It explores themes of renewal, the cyclical nature of life, and the fleeting beauty of moments

Than the joys of night

And are the vigrous joys of youth

Ashamed of the light

The vineyards in the night
