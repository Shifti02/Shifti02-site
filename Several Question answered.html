Analysis

Several Questions answered

The Poem is about questions answered from Blake's perspective. The First two are desires of humans, the second stanza says that sometimes it's soft deceit that wins a lover instead of the true love. Soft Deceit and idleness are superficial and can sometimes decieve the thruth, while for the joy it's better to embrace it to live in eternity and happiness instead of possessing it and ruining it.

What is it men in women do require?

The lineaments of Gratified Desire.

What is it women do in men require?

The lineaments of Gratified Desire.

The look of love alarms

Because 'tis fill'd with fire;

But the look of soft deceit

Shall Win the lover's hire.

Soft Deceit & Idleness,

These are Beauty's sweetest dress.

He who binds to himself a joy

Dot the winged life destroy;

But he who kisses the joy as it flies

Lives in Eternity's sunrise.
