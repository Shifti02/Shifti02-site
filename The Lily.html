Analysis

The Lily

The rose, while beautiful, has thorns. This suggests that even beauty has its defenses and potential for harm. The sheep, often seen as a symbol of innocence, has horns which can be seen as a means of protection.

The modest Rose puts forth a thorn,

The humble sheep a threat'ning horn:

While the Lily white shall in love delight,

Nor a thorn nor a threat stain her beauty bright.
