All Religions are one

The central thesis of Blake's work is encapsulated in its title, "All Religions are One." Blake asserts that all religions, despite their outward differences, share a common foundation rooted in the human soul and its divine origins. Blake's idea challenges the exclusivity of any single religion and promotes a universal spirituality
