Analysis

I Asked a thief to steal me a peach

This poem is a dark comedy that explores the themes of innocence, desire, and deception. The speaker asks a thief to a peach, but the thief refuses. He then asks a woman to lay down with him, and she does. However, the woman is in league with the thief, and they rob the speaker after he falls asleep

I asked a thief to steal me a peach: He turn'd up his eyes. I ask'd a lithe lady to lie her down: Holy and meek she cries. As soon as I went an angel came: He wink'd at the thief And smil'd at the dame, And without one word spoke Had a peach from the tree, And 'twixt earnest and joke Enjoy'd the Lady
