The Four Zoas: Chapter i

Chapter I sets the stage for the cosmic struggle among the Four Zoas, who are personifications of the fundamental aspects of the human soul. These Zoas are: Tharmas - representing the body and primal instincts. Urthona (Los) - representing imagination and creativity. Urizen - representing reason and law. Luvah (Orc) - representing love and emotion.
